---
question: "What are the internet speed requirements for epix iptv?"
category: "technical"
order: 5
---

For optimal viewing quality with epix iptv, a minimum internet speed of 5 Mbps is recommended for standard definition content. For high-definition (HD) and 4K Ultra HD content, higher speeds of at least 15 Mbps to 25 Mbps are advisable. Ensuring a stable and high-speed internet connection will guarantee a seamless and uninterrupted viewing experience for users across the United States.
