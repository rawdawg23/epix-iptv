---
question: "Can I use epix iptv on multiple devices?"
category: "general"
order: 6
---

Yes, epix iptv supports multi-device usage, allowing users in the United States to stream content on multiple devices simultaneously. Depending on the chosen subscription package, the number of devices that can simultaneously access the service may vary. This feature makes it convenient for families or individuals who wish to watch different content on different screens at the same time.
